GETTING TO PHONG NHA
The Area

Logistics

Travel options to Phong Nha

Pick your own travel options to get to Phong Nha Town, where most likely you are going to rent your accommodation. You can either use private cars, sleeping bus, train, or flights.

Flights

You are going to land at Dong Hoi airport which is around 40km South from Phong Nha. From here you can use Jungle Boss private car service, or catch a taxi at the airport.

Train

Arrived at Dong Hoi train station. You can use Jungle Boss private car service, catch a taxi at the airport, or use Dong Hoi - Phong Nha bus (one in every 1 hour).

Sleeping buses

There are several sleeping buses from along the country to Phong Nha. Most popular is from Hue, Da Nang or Ninh Binh, Ha Noi.

Transfers

We offer FREE 2-way transportation within Phong Nha and Dong Hoi before and after the tour
